Welcome!

All that Jazz was created for the musician, by a musician.

Nirayka Monga noticed how inconvenient it was to carry around 500-page sheet music books to gigs and performances. She decided to take matters into her own hands and built All That Jazz using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Instead of having to flip through the unwieldy Real Book, you can visit this web app and access 100+ lead sheets for popular jazz standards. The app is functional on all devices, from mobile smartphones to desktop computers, so you can visit the site anytime, anywhere. And, unlike with the physical Real Book, All That Jazz allows you to organize lead sheets by genre, tempo, key, and time signature, making it easier to hone in on certain skills.
